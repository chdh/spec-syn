Vowel primitives collection

Methodology

The SpecSyn samples in this collection are grouped in the following way:
  • Originals
    • Based on recordings of the ZHCorpus collection.
    • Spectrum, frequency and amplitude curves automatically generated by the SpecSyn analysis function.
  • Conservative primitives
    • Spectrum slightly reduced and simplified.
    • Mostly the same sound as the original.
  • Radical primitives
    • Spectrum further reduced.
    • Singer's formant (Squillo) removed or merged.
    • Simple forms are preferred in the hope of showing the archetypal spectral shape.

Tip: Use the space bar to play a sound after opening a SpecSyn link.
